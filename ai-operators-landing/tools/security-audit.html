What does this do?

This prompt unleashes Claude as your personal security auditor. It systematically checks your firewall settings, scans for suspicious processes, reviews app permissions, examines network configurations, and identifies potential attack vectors. You get actionable fixes—not just warnings. Think of it as hiring a senior penetration tester for the cost of an API call.
